XIAN
XIAN is a scalable, developer-centric blockchain platform empowering developers to create & deploy smart contracts written in Python.

This project is in many ways the spiritual successor to Lamden, a project from the 2017 ICO era, loved and anticipated by many which ultimately threw in the towel before delivering on it's vision.

It is founded and maintained by many of the builders and community from Lamden who fell in love with the ease-of-use and simplicity of Lamden's tooling & smart-contracts.
